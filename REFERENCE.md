# Reference — external award-grade study (bin store / discount / variety)

Researched fresh for this build. The bar is borrowed (cohesion, editorial type
ambition, restraint, one confident moment), never the shapes.

## What the category does (the cliché we reject)
Bin-store and discount-store branding online is near-universally LOUD: red/yellow
starbursts, "SALE!!!" clip art, neon price-explosion graphics, cluttered grids of
mismatched product photos, dollar-sign confetti. It reads as chaos and cheapness.
Source scan: TikTok/FB pages of NJ bin stores (Orange Crazy Hot Deals, generic
"bin store near me" listings), shopbinstores.com, liquidation-store guides.

## The award-grade direction we borrow the BAR from
- **Editorial / gallery e-commerce** (Awwwards e-commerce + SiteInspire): calm
  warm-paper grounds, large symmetrical serif display, generous whitespace,
  one accent, type-as-hero. Cinematic pacing, restrained motion.
- **Monochrome editorial product sites** (e.g. timepiece/atelier studio work cited
  on DesignRush Aug-2025, StudioBamo.J Nagarā): refined, museum-quiet, editorial
  polish — the inverse of discount chaos. We apply that quiet to a $1 bin store,
  which is the whole joke and the whole novelty.

## The single idea where brand-truth meets a technique
Brand truth = "the price drops every day." Technique = an interactive value-decay
dial the visitor scrubs through the week. A quiet, premium STAGE around one loud
mechanic. That tension (gallery calm + treasure-hunt thrill) is the concept.

## Concrete moves taken
- Gambetta (warm contemporary serif, real italics) as display; Manrope body.
- Centered symmetrical composition, big negative space, hairline center rules.
- Pine-teal (#2C8C7D) reserved to mark TODAY and the live price — color = signal.
- Warm gallery paper ground (#F4F0E7), ink type — not a single neon starburst.
- One bespoke energy band (slow marquee of real category words) instead of a
  loud confetti banner.
- Real photography of bin-store / treasure-hunt retail, graded to one warm wash.

## Imagery plan (community-true, Orange NJ Main St)
Real free-stock of overstock/variety retail and the dig-through-bins moment,
people-light and product/hands-forward to avoid wrong-community casting; one warm
grade unifies the set. Flagged for swap with the owner's own restock photos.
